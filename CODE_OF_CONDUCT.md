# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus criticism on ideas, not people.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Accept feedback and collaborate in good faith.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, discrimination, and deliberate disruption.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and maintainers' review interactions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or restrict participation for behavior that violates this policy.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately to `info@sd-group.com.au`.
